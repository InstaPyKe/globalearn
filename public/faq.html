Help & Knowledge Base

Everything you need to know about working on GlobalEarn.

Payment Cycles
When and how do I get paid?
We process withdrawals within 24 to 48 hours. You can request a payout via M-PESA or PayPal once you reach the minimum threshold of **KSH. 500.00**.
Note: High-tier members (Gold/Platinum) enjoy priority instant processing.
Are there any withdrawal fees?
Standard accounts incur a 5% processing fee to cover blockchain gas and transaction costs. Platinum members enjoy 0% fees on all withdrawals.
Account Rules
Can I have multiple accounts?
Strictly No. To maintain fairness and prevent fraud, our system uses AI-based device fingerprinting. If multiple accounts are detected from the same device or IP without prior authorization, all associated accounts will be permanently banned.
How does the 3-Level Referral system work?
You earn from your direct invites (Level 1), their invites (Level 2), and the third tier (Level 3).
  • Level 1: 10% of their earnings
  • Level 2: 5% of their earnings
  • Level 3: 2% of their earnings
Referral commissions are credited instantly as soon as your downline completes a task.
Troubleshooting
My video task is not rewarding me. Why?
This usually happens if: 1. You closed the tab before the timer finished. 2. You used an Ad-Blocker (please disable it for our site). 3. You are using a VPN or Proxy. Ensure you watch the full duration and click "Submit" to secure your reward.
I forgot my 2FA code. How do I recover it?
For security reasons, 2FA resets require manual review. Please contact our support team via the Contact Us page with your KYC ID document ready for identity verification.
